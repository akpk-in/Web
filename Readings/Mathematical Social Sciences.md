06. Mathematical Tools for Social Scientists by Fionn Murtagh and Adam Kurz Covers essential mathematical techniques for social science research.
07. Quantitative Sociology: International Perspectives on Mathematical and Statistical Modeling edited by H. M. Blalock Provides an international perspective on mathematical modeling in sociology.
08. Mathematical Models in Social and Cognitive Sciences by Serge Galam Explores mathematical models across various social and cognitive domains.
